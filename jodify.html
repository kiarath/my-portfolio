Jodify – Electronic Music App

Design and launch of a native mobile application to help electronic music fans discover and connect with local music events in Latin America.

What is JODIFY?

JODIFY is an application designed to fill a gap in the Latin American market: it centralizes all available electronic music events near the user. While other solutions like RA (Resident Advisor), DICE, and Shotgun exist globally, they are not available in Latin America, leaving electronic music fans without a dedicated platform to discover events.

Why a Native App?

Currently, JODIFY only exists as a web application. This project details the process of adapting and redesigning the application to meet the necessary requirements for publication on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, while providing users with compelling reasons to download the native app rather than continuing to use the web version.

Context

The electronic music scene is at its historical peak. In 2024, Latin America led the world in revenue growth, with Mexico and Brazil ranking among the top 10 countries for streaming this genre. Globally, electronic music streams have surpassed 10 billion.

The Problem

Despite this growth, finding specific electronic music events in Latin America is a significant challenge, especially for subgenres. There is no centralized solution available in Latin America for discovering all electronic music events near you.

01. User Research

While several user personas were used during development, "Martina" was the foundational persona for creating new features and guiding design decisions.

Meet Martina

• 26 years old• University Student• Good technological skill level

Martina is a student who recently started going to electronic music parties. Due to a lack of time, she can only go once or twice a month, so she wants to make the most of her choice. She actively investigates and seeks recommendations from acquaintances to decide where to go.

Motivations:

  • • Finding high-quality parties that match her music taste
  • • Making the most of her limited free time
  • • Discovering new subgenres and artists

Frustrations:

  • • Struggles to find parties using Instagram or Google
  • • Events very far away requiring extra transportation
  • • Endless scrolling without clear filtering options
02. Requirements & Objectives

Primary Objective

Adapt the existing JODIFY web application to meet the necessary requirements for publication on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Secondary Objective

Provide users with compelling reasons to download the native app rather than continuing to use the web app, addressing Martina's core frustrations with a fast, centralized, and location-aware experience.

Key User Needs

Speed

Users need to find relevant events quickly without endless scrolling.

Filtering

Advanced filters for genre, subgenre, location, and date are essential.

Location Clarity

Event location must be immediately visible to address transportation concerns.

Native Features

Take advantage of mobile capabilities like notifications and location services.

04. Validation & Testing

To validate the new design, we conducted a round of moderated usability testing with 5 participants who matched our persona's profile.

Hypothetical Scenario

"It's Tuesday, and you want to plan your weekend. You're in the mood for a 'Techno' party on Saturday, but you don't have a car and want to find something in your area."

Testing Tasks

Task 1: "Find a 'Techno' event for this Saturday."

Objective: Test the usability of the new search, filtering, and "Tag" components.

Success Criterion: User successfully applies filters and identifies a relevant event.

Task 2: "From the results, find an event located in 'Palermo'."

Objective: Validate that the new event "Cards" present key information clearly.

Success Criterion: User correctly identifies a local event without clicking into the details page.

Results

Success Rate: 100% (5/5) of users successfully completed both tasks.

Task 1: Find a "Techno" event

Result: 100% SUCCESS.

All users successfully used the filter system to find relevant Techno events.

Task 2: Find an event in "Palermo"

Result: 100% SUCCESS.

All users identified the location information directly from the event cards without needing to click through to details.

Time on Task

Users found a relevant event in an average of 40 seconds, a significant improvement over the benchmarked 3-5 minutes of "endless scrolling" on Instagram or Google.

Qualitative Feedback

"This is exactly what I need. Seeing the subgenre and the neighborhood on the card saves so much time."

"The filters are simple and clear. On other apps, I never know if I'm looking at events for this week or next month."

"I would definitely download this over using the website. It feels faster."

Conclusion

The testing confirmed that the new design successfully addresses the core frustrations of our target user, providing a fast, centralized, and clear way to find events.

05. The Final Solution

The result is the JODIFY native app for iOS and Android. The new design features a bottom navigation bar (Home, Search, Profile), a prominent event carousel, and a clean, filterable list of upcoming events, all unified by the new design system and ready for launch.

Key Features

Smart Filtering

Advanced filters for genre, subgenre, date, and location help users find exactly what they're looking for.

Location Tags

Clear neighborhood tags on every event card address transportation concerns.

Featured Carousel

Highlighted events and recommendations at the top of the feed.

Native Experience

Fast performance, push notifications, and seamless integration with iOS and Android.
